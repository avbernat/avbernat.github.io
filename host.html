Host Plant Ecology

What happens to the native community?


When organisms adapt to novel environments, this does not happen in a vacuum. We study how gene flow back to populations using the ancestral environment impacts their ability to persist and compete, and how this shifts the evolutionary pressure they exert in the native community.
