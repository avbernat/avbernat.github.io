Research

Flight Dispersal
We aim to better understand the drivers and changes in flight dispersal among various insect species. We use a theoretical simulations and a flight mill machine with 3D printed enhancements to explore dispersability.
Reproductive Plasticity
Understanding the mechanisms or barriers that keep species or locally adapted populations distinct is a critical aspect of speciation research. We explore how flexible different types of reproductive barriers are.
Host Plant Ecology
We research how species‐specific and age‐varying host‐plant traits affect the growth and survival of its insect host.
Insect Nutrition
We map host plant distributions through time using herbarium records. These mappings help us witness the emergence and collapse of local adaption over several decades.
Insect Morphology
We record and describe the physical form of insects using microscopy and Java-based image processing programs.
