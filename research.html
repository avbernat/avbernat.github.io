Research

Flight Dispersal
We aim to better understand the drivers and changes in flight dispersal among various insect species (collected from environments exhibiting accelerating climate change and habitat fragmentation). We use a flight mill machine that we have built and continue updating using laser cutting and 3D printing techniques.
Reproductive Plasticity
Understanding the mechanisms or barriers that keep species or locally adapted populations distinct is one of the critical aspects of speciation research. We explore how flexible different types of reproductive barriers are.
