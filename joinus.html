Join us



We are currently recruiting graduate and undergraduate students broadly interested in the evolution and ecology of insects. Undergraduates can join the lab as employees or for course credit. You do not need to have prior lab experience. Due to the COVID pandemic, our lab is currently operating remotely but is still open to recruiting remote members. We conduct entirely computational projects using R and Python, so all lab members are supported and encouraged to develop the quantitative and computational tools available to ecologists.


If you are interested in working in these exciting areas, contact Meredith Cenzer directly via email describing why you are interested in joining the lab. A CV or resume is also very helpful. We particularly welcome applicants from backgrounds historically and currently underrepresented in STEM. We encourage you to look on Handshake for job ads – but if you don’t see one yet, don’t let that stop you from reaching out!
