Join us



We welcome undergraduate students broadly interested in the evolution and ecology of insects. Undergraduates can join the lab as employees or for course credit. You do not need to have prior lab experience; all you need is an openness to learn and a willingness to handle live insects! All lab members are supported and encouraged to develop the quantitative and computational tools available to ecologists.


If you are interested in working in these exciting areas, contact Meredith Cenzer directly via email describing why you are interested in joining the lab. A CV or resume is helpful, but not necessary. We particularly welcome applicants from backgrounds historically and currently underrepresented in STEM. We encourage you to look on Handshake for job ads – but if you don’t see one yet, don’t let that stop you from reaching out!
